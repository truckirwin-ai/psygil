## Head-to-Head Positioning

### vs. PsychAssist (closest competitor)
**They claim:** End-to-end AI-native platform with Daubert/Frye support
**We counter:** Claims without architecture. PsychAssist has no gate-based workflow, no peer review module, no UNID redaction, and no published assessment list. Psygil's forensic support is built into the architecture, not bolted onto marketing copy.
**Key differentiator:** Gate-based workflow + peer review + local-first PHI

### vs. Psynth (strongest privacy competitor)
**They claim:** 370+ assessments, zero-retention LLMs, triple compliance (HIPAA/GDPR/PIPEDA)
**We counter:** Assessment breadth without forensic depth. Psynth has no forensic workflow, no gate decisions, no peer review. Their zero-retention LLMs are a policy claim; Psygil's local-first architecture is an engineering guarantee.
**Key differentiator:** Forensic-native architecture + UNID redaction (stronger than zero-retention)

### vs. PAR AI Report Writer (sleeping giant)
**They claim:** Built on PAR's rigorous quality standards, PARiConnect integration
**We counter:** Their EULA trains AI on your patient data. For forensic psychologists handling legally privileged case information, this is an ethical liability. Psygil's local-first architecture makes data training architecturally impossible.
**Key differentiator:** Data privacy commitment vs. data training EULA

### vs. PsychReport.ai (transparent pricing competitor)
**They claim:** 145+ assessments, 80% time reduction, transparent pricing
**We counter:** No forensic support, no legal defensibility framing, no peer review. Good for general clinical; insufficient for forensic or high-stakes evaluations.
**Key differentiator:** Forensic workflow + audit trail + peer review

### vs. FT Nexus (legacy forensic incumbent)
**They claim:** Desktop forensic tool with offender normative databases
**We counter:** Legacy technology, desktop-bound, limited instruments. FT Nexus has the forensic pedigree but the technology is outdated. Psygil modernizes forensic evaluation without sacrificing the depth that forensic practitioners need.
**Key differentiator:** Modern AI-powered workflow vs. legacy scoring automation
