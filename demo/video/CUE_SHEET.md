## Recording Tips

- Use Screen Studio or QuickTime for screen capture
- Set resolution to 1920x1080 or native display resolution
- Hide the macOS dock and menu bar for a cleaner look
- Keep the app window maximized
- Practice the click sequence once before recording
- Each transition should be a clean click, no mouse wandering
