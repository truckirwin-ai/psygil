Creating a Case

Start a new evaluation. Psygil opens a six-step wizard, scaffolds the case folder, and seeds every document you will eventually need.

Open the wizard

In the left column, click New Case. The Intake and Onboarding wizard opens at step 1.

The six steps

  1. Contact and Insurance. Name, DOB, contact information, emergency contact, insurance carrier. Phone numbers auto-format as you type (1112223333 becomes (111) 222-3333). Clicking Save and Continue here is what creates the case folder and all subfolders on disk.
  2. Referral and Legal. Referring party, court and attorney details, evaluation type, charges, deadlines.
  3. Demographics and Family. Gender, marital status, employment, education, languages, family history.
  4. Presenting Complaints. Chief complaint, onset, severity.
  5. Medical and Substance Use. Medical history, medications, substance use patterns.
  6. Recent Events. Incidents or stressors relevant to the evaluation.
Save as you go. Every step autosaves when you advance or click Save. You can close the wizard and resume later; Psygil reopens at the last saved step.

What gets created on disk

As soon as you finish step 1, Psygil creates a case folder named like 2026-0001 Doe, Jane with seven subfolders: _Inbox, Collateral, Testing, Interviews, Diagnostics, Reports, Archive. Each subfolder has a short README and, where useful, a stub log file you can fill in.

What happens after step 6

The case advances from the Onboarding stage to the Testing stage. It appears in the left column with a stage badge. Click the case to open its workspace.
