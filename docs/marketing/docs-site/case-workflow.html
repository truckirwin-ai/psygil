Case Workflow

Six stages, three gates, one source of truth.

The six stages

  1. Onboarding (intake and patient history)
  2. Testing (administer instruments, record scores)
  3. Interview (clinical and collateral interviews)
  4. Diagnostics (differential reasoning, formulation)
  5. Review (report draft and final review)
  6. Complete (published, archived)

Testing

  1. Select the Testing tab for the open case.
  2. Add instruments to the battery. Psygil pre-suggests batteries by evaluation type.
  3. Upload raw protocol PDFs to the Testing folder, or drag them into the tab.
  4. Enter scored values. Validity scales flag automatically.
  5. Fill Battery Selection, Validity and Effort, and Testing Observations notes.
  6. When all required instruments are complete, Gate 1 is satisfied. Advance the case to Interview.

Interview

  1. Record Behavioral Observations, Mental Status Exam, Collateral, and Free Notes in the right-rail panel.
  2. Attach collateral interview notes to the Interviews folder. Click any document to open it as a tab.
  3. Advance to Diagnostics when the interview record is captured.

Diagnostics

See the dedicated Diagnostics page. In short: select or rule out each candidate diagnosis, write and approve the four Final Formulation sections, sign the attestation, and click Approve and Build Report.

Review and Complete

The Report tab populates progressively as stages complete. When you are satisfied, click Publish Final. Psygil writes locked Word and PDF files to the Reports folder, records an audit hash, and moves earlier drafts to Archive.

The doctor always diagnoses. No gate advances without explicit clinician action. Psygil will never auto-approve a formulation.
