Reports and Publishing

Export to Word and PDF. Publish when the record is locked.

Progressive drafting

The Report tab fills in progressively as each stage completes. Before a stage is complete, that section shows a clear placeholder. This guarantees your report is never ahead of your clinical work.

Export to Word

  1. Open the Report tab for the case.
  2. Click Export → DOCX.
  3. Psygil writes a versioned draft (draft_v1.docx, draft_v2.docx, and so on) into the case's Reports folder.
  4. Open the file in Word, Pages, or LibreOffice to review.

Export to PDF

  1. Click Export → PDF.
  2. Choose a filename and location.
  3. Psygil renders the report and writes a PDF with Letter page size and 0.75 inch margins.

Publish Final

  1. Confirm that all gates are passed and the attestation is signed.
  2. Click Publish Final.
  3. Psygil writes locked Word and PDF files to Reports/final/, records a SHA-256 hash in the audit log, moves earlier drafts to Archive, and marks the case Complete.
Final means final. Once published, the files are read-only and their hashes are stamped into the audit log. To revise, a practice admin must create a new draft cycle; the original stays intact for the record.

What is in a report

  • Cover page with case number, clinician, date, evaluation type.
  • Reason for referral, informed consent record.
  • Background and history drawn from onboarding.
  • Testing summary, instrument by instrument, with validity notes.
  • Interview summary.
  • Diagnostic impressions and rule-outs in your words.
  • Prognosis and recommendations.
  • Attestation block with clinician signature line.
