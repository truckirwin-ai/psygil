# Psygil Demo Recording Guide

## What's Ready

- **`psygil_demo_voiceover.mp3`** (2:19) - Complete voiceover, Eric voice (American, conversational)
- **`psygil_demo_with_voiceover.mp4`** (2:23) - Video with title card + voiceover + end card (screen area is black, needs your recording)
- **`frames/title_card.png`** + **`frames/end_card.png`** - Title and end cards

## How to Record

1. Open Screen Studio (or QuickTime > File > New Screen Recording)
2. Set capture to the Psygil app window only
3. Play `psygil_demo_voiceover.mp3` through headphones
4. Follow the cue sheet below, clicking at each timestamp
5. Export your recording
6. In iMovie/Final Cut/CapCut: drop your screen recording between the title card (3s) and end card (5s)
7. Lay the voiceover audio track underneath

## Quick Cuts (2-3 seconds)
Shots 3, 7, 8, 12 are quick flashes. Click and move on immediately.

## Longer Holds (5-8 seconds)
Shots 1, 4, 10, 14 are hero shots. Let the viewer absorb the screen.

## Tips
- Keep mouse movements smooth and deliberate
- Click targets precisely (no hunting)
- Let each screen settle for 0.5s before moving to the next
- The voiceover guides the pacing; follow its rhythm
